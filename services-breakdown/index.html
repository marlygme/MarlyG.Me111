Custom Coded Websites

Contact for Pricing

For clients seeking maximum flexibility and performance, I also offer fully custom coded websites built from the ground up (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). This approach offers unique benefits and trade-offs compared to platform-based sites like Shopify or Squarespace.

Hosting & Ongoing Costs

  • Lower Cost: Hosting for custom-coded sites is often significantly cheaper than platform subscription fees, especially for non-e-commerce projects.
  • Client Responsibility: You will be responsible for securing your own hosting plan (I can provide recommendations).

Website Edits & Ownership

  • Developer-Led Edits: By default, all changes and updates to a custom-coded website are made by a developer (me).
  • No User Interface (UI): Unlike Shopify or Squarespace, a standard custom-coded website does not have a built-in visual editor for you to make changes yourself.
  • Full Ownership: If you ever request a transfer of ownership, you will receive a complete folder of the website's code files. Managing these files requires technical expertise.

Advanced SEO Audit & Strategy

An SEO Audit is a comprehensive health check for your website's visibility on search engines like Google. I will perform a deep analysis of your site's technical performance, content, and authority, delivering a clear, prioritised action plan to help you rank higher and attract more organic traffic.

Pricing & Tiers

(Prices vary based on website size and complexity)

Tier 1: On-Page & Technical SEO Audit

$550

This audit is perfect for businesses that want to ensure their website is technically sound and fully optimised.

  • Google Analytics & Search Console Analysis: Reviewing traffic, user behaviour, and keyword performance
  • Technical SEO Health Check: Checking for indexing issues, site speed, mobile-friendliness, and broken links
  • On-Page Content Review: Analysing metadata (titles, descriptions), alt text, keyword usage, and content structure
  • Action Plan: A detailed report outlining all findings and a prioritised checklist of recommended changes

Tier 2: Comprehensive Audit + Backlink Strategy

$950

This tier includes everything in Tier 1, plus a deep dive into your site's authority and competitive landscape.

  • Backlink Profile Analysis: A full review of all existing links to your site to identify toxic or low-quality links that could be harming your ranking
  • Competitor Backlink Analysis: Identifying the high-quality links your top competitors have that you don't
  • Backlink Strategy Development: A strategic plan outlining actionable steps and opportunities for acquiring high-authority backlinks to boost your domain authority

What's Not Included

Implementation of Changes: This service provides the audit and strategic plan only. The work to implement the recommended changes on your website will be quoted separately as a new project.
